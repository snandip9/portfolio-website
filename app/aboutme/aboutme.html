Manifesto
— □ ×

The creation of this website is heavily inspired by Neocities and the Indie Web movement; it is one of the few remnants of a free web that we have left today. The internet should be a place of community and creativity and sometimes it does not feel like that anymore. Too many companies nowadays have made their priority not to sell a useful and innovative product but to keep you on their platform long enough to harvest your data.

This website is an homage to the days when people would hand code their websites by leanring little bits of HTML and CSS. This is a place to showcase some of my projects and ocassionally post some opinions on tech and the wider society. Everything has been coded by myself using HTML, CSS, JS and using the Angular Framework. This is purely for fun and to make my own space on the internet as people before me have done :D
